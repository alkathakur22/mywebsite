Get to know me

The story so far

Every career has a moment when the work stops being about the work. When the spreadsheet is fine, the strategy is sound, and yet the room does not move. That is the moment I work in.

I spent my early years inside a technology company, watching brilliant people go quiet in rooms that needed them most. Then I stepped out, built an independent practice, and spent the next decade helping professionals across India speak, lead and listen with intention.

My work rests on a simple belief. Communication is not a talent you are born with. It is a craft you can practise. Workshops, facilitation, Train the Trainer programmes, one to one consulting. Different rooms, same purpose. Helping people show up as themselves, only steadier.

When I am not in a training room, I am usually writing, reflecting, or sitting with a cup of tea that has gone cold because the sentence mattered more.
